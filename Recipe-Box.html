Crepes recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 3/4 cup of flour
  • 1/4 tsp of baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp of salt
  • 1/4 stick of butter to bake

Instructions:

    Take bowl. Crack 1 egg. Add milk. Wisk.
    Add 1/4 tsp of baking soda and salt. Wisk. It's ready to cook.
    Preheat a pan to medium temperature. Put some butter in the pan.
    Pour some batter and swirl. Cook untill golden brown.
